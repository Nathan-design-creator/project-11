Best Travel Destinations

1: Alaska.

Due to the cold weather, you should definitely carry jackets and sweaters for warmness.

2: California Beaches.

It has a little warm climate so do not carry any heavy clothes. It is recommended to wear shirts and shorts.

3: Colorado Hills.

You should wear a pair of boots and also carry a hat due to windy situations caused by high altitude.

4: Paris, France.

You should carry different pair of clothes. Just mix them up i.e jackets, sweaters, shirts and shorts due to the different weather conditions.
